Instructions

This is a simulated ekg machine. to obtian a ekg on your patient follow these steps:

  1. Take machine to the bedside.
  2. On the home screen page, click 'New'
  3. Locate your patient in the list, old results may also show so choose the newest one visible
    You are not required to apply leads in simulation
  4. The ekg will show on a new page.
  5. Call the provider with the results.
